About

The Derby City Cinema Draft is a fantasy league started back in Summer of 2013. We were inspired by Brian Brushwood and Diamond Club's Movie Draft League The basic idea is fantasy sports for the cinephile!

Every year (or "season"), we each get ₪100 to spend on a play auction. We gather over beer and pizza, and settle in for a couple hours of movie trailers. After each trailer, we all bid on the movie, with various strategies used to try and predict the movies that will make the most money for the new "owner."
